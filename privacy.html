Privacy Policy

Last revision: April 11, 2025

1. Introduction

At TrueFort, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Minecraft server and website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Email address, Minecraft username, and IP address.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our server and website, including login times, gameplay statistics, and website navigation patterns.
  • Communication Data: Information you provide when contacting us through our contact form or other communication channels.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Providing and maintaining our services
  • Improving our services
  • Communicating with you about server updates, events, and other relevant information
  • Enforcing our server rules and Terms of Use
  • Addressing technical issues and providing support

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6. Your Rights

Depending on updates in the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania's laws, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to object to processing of your personal information
  • The right to data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us through our contact form.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Third-Party Services

Our website and server may use third-party services that collect information. These third parties have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

9. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact form.
