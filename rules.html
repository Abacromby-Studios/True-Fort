Rules and Regulations

Please follow these guidelines to ensure a positive experience for everyone

At TrueFort, we strive to maintain a friendly, safe, and enjoyable environment for all players. By joining our server, you agree to abide by the following rules:

General Rules

  1. Rule #1: No DDoS, or DoS attacks, stress tests, boots or otherwise attempt to disrupt or damage a computer network or machine.
  2. Rule #2: No disclosure of your (or somebody else's) Personally Identifiable Information.
  3. Rule #3: No violation of real world laws (jurisdiction is United States of America, Commonwealth of Pennsylvania.

Chat Rules

  1. Rule #4: No spamming/chat walls.
  2. Rule #5: No advertisements
  3. Rule #6: No disrespecting members, players, Policy Enforcement Team members, Development Team members, or Management.
  4. Rule #7: No racism, discrimination, homophobia, transphobia, xenophobia, sexism, or otherwise using language in a way intended to demean or harass a person or group of people.
  5. Rule 8: No Harassing Members, Players, Policy Enforcement Team Members, Development Team Members, Management Team Members, or anyone else not listed.
  6. Rule 9: No NSFW, NSFL, Illegal, Excessively violant, or otherwise sexual, illegal, Excessively violant, or shock content.

Gameplay Rules

  1. Rule #10: No using scripts, hacks, cheats, clients, software, or other files that inject data into Minecraft or the server to manipulate data to give you an unfair advantage.
  2. Rule #11: No exploitation of any known or unknown bugs on server-side or Minecraft-side.
  3. Rule #12: No duplication exploits.
  4. Rule #12l3: No extreme toxicity.
  5. Rule #14: No ERPing, NSFW, NSFL, illegal or otherwise shock or sexual content.

Building Rules

  1. Rule #15: No builds that are intended or unintended to cause serious strain on server infrastructure.
  2. Rule #17: No political builds.
  3. Rule #18: No racist, discriminatory, homophobic, transphobic, xenophobic, furryphobic, or other builds intended to offend or otherwise demean a person or group of persons.
  4. Rule #19: No sexually suggestive, or outright sexual builds, for example, a sheep being raped in the ass by a redstone machine.

These rules are subject to change at any time. It is your responsibility to stay updated with the current rules. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse for breaking them.

If you have any questions about the rules, please contact a staff member on our Discord server.
