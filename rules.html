Rules and Regulations

Please follow these guidelines to ensure a positive experience for everyone

At True-Fort SMP & Factions, we strive to maintain a friendly, safe, and enjoyable environment for all players. By joining our server, you agree to abide by the following rules:

General Rules

  1. Rule #1: No DDoSing, DoSing, Stress Testing, or other forms of disrupting network infrastructure.
  2. Rule #2: No leaking Personally Identifiable Information or otherwise doxxing.
  3. Rule #3: No utilization of alternative accounts, whatever the reason is, this includes but is bot limited to: to evade enforcement actions, for gameplay advantages etc.

Chat Rules

  1. Rule #4: No Spamming/chat flooding.
  2. Rule #5: No chat walls.
  3. Rule #6: No advertisements.
  4. Rule #7: No harassment and/or disrespecting players.
  5. Rule #8: No racism, discrimination, homophobia, transphobia, xenophobia, etc.

Gameplay Rules

    Building Rules

    1. Rule #14: No building builds intended to damage, slow, crash, or lag the servers infrastructures performance or Minecraft as a whole.
    2. Rule #15: No building NSFW, NSFL, Sexual, Illegal or other builds intended to shock players.
    3. Rule #16: No sexually explicit redstone builds, or builds in general.

Additional Rules

  1. Rule #17: No ERPing.
  2. Rule #18: No attempting to disclose player account credentials or attempt to gain access to a players account.
  3. Rule #19: No Combat-Logging or C-Logging.
  4. Rule #20: No false reporting players with malicious intentions.

These rules are subject to change at any time. It is your responsibility to stay updated with the current rules. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse for breaking them.

If you have any questions about the rules, please contact a staff member on our Discord server.
